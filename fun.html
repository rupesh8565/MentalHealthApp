Fun Learning Activities

Engage in these fun and interactive activities to boost your mental well-being while having fun!

This simple activity helps improve your mood. Try writing down three things you're grateful for today.

How to Play: Think about what makes you happy, and reflect on how it makes you feel better.

Memory Matching Game

Sharpen your memory with this simple matching game. Try to remember the sequence of calming images and recreate them in order.

How to Play: Start with a few items, and try to recall them. Increase difficulty by adding more items as you get better.
